Storytime for Toddlers


Date: Every Tuesday, 10.30 am - 11:00 am
Location: Children's Section
Age Group: 1 - 3 years old

Join us every Tuesday morning for a fun and engaging Storytime for Toddlers! This interactive event is designed to introduce young children to the joy of reading and foster a love of books from an early age. Our experienced children's librarians will lead the session with a variety of activities that are perfect for toddlers.

Book Author Talk


Book Author: Jane Smith
Date: 22 July 2024
Time: 6:00 pm - 7:30 pm

Join us for an exciting evening with bestselling author Jane Smith as she discusses her latest novel, "Whispers of the Past." This is a unique opportunity to gain insight into the creative process of a celebrated writer, ask questions and get your books signed.

Library Fine Amnesty Month


Dates: 1 - 31 August 2024
Location: Library Main Counter
Cost: Free

August is Library Fine Amnesty Month! This is your chance to return overdue library materials without having to pay any late fees. Whether you've been avoiding the library because of fines or simply forgot to return a book, we welcome you back with open arms. Take advantage of this special month-long event to clear your account.
